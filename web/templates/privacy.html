Privacy Policy

Bitusenet respects each individual user's right to privacy. We have created this Privacy Statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. This Privacy Statement discloses our information-gathering and dissemination practices. Bitusenet fully respects your privacy rights, and our policies reflect our commitment to your privacy.

Customer Information

Bitusenet requires certain information at the time of signup and in the course of providing customer support. This information is necessary for us to provide quality service and support. This information is never released to others, unless we are ordered by a court of competent jurisdiction to do so. Bitusenet has never sold or rented customer lists or any other customer information, and Bitusenet will never do so.

Bitusenet monitors our servers to the extent necessary to ensure that high standards of maintenance are met. Bitusenet does not monitor or record your activities online. We do not monitor which newsgroups you post to or download from or what you put in news articles that you post.

Usage Information

Bitusenet will not release a customer's personal information or usage information to investigators, attorneys, or agencies unless we are directed to do so by a court of competent jurisdiction in the matter. If there is a hearing in court, the customer will be notified so they will have an opportunity to contest the surrender of personal information.

Civil Investigations

Bitusenet cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies, yet there must still be a court order before Bitusenet surrenders customer information. The Fourth Amendment to the US Constitution requires a court order to conduct a search and seizure. Bitusenet will, when requested by law enforcement entities, acknowledge the existence of personal customer information, and when requested, provide the technical language to include in the court order, "particularly describing ... the property to be seized." In a criminal investigation Bitusenet is under a duty not to divulge the fact of the investigation to the customer

Criminal Investigations

Bitusenet holds personal information about you in the strictest confidence and does not sell or rent that information. Bitusenet will not release or divulge any customer information unless ordered to do so by a court of law.
