Do you accept any other form of payment other than bitcoin?

Yes, crypto currencies only though. We now accept Litecoin, Dogecoin, Darkcoin, Maxcoin, Peercoin, Vertcoin, Razorcoin, Vericoin. More are being added all the time.

How long does it take to activate my account?

Seconds. Our backend runs off of 0 confirmations on all the coin networks. As soon as you send the coins, you'll be able to start using the usenet server.
